generator

Port - the specific port located on the card
Status - the current state on the port
Streams - the number of streams defined on the port
Port Type - the type of connection used on the port

stream view

Port - the specific port located on the card
ID - the identification number of a stream
Flows - the number of flows defined on the stream
Enabled - specifies if the stream should be used for traffic generation
Packet Type - the type of packet defined on a stream
Destination MAC Address - the MAC address of the device where packets will be sent
Source MAC Address - the MAC address of the device where packets will be generated
