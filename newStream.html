Create a Stream

A stream is defined as the connection between the locations (source and destination) which send/receive traffic in the form of packets. Packets are sent from a source location (source MAC address) to a desitination location (destination MAC address), also referred to as traffic generation. The format of the packets can vary depending on particular protocols, but the basic structure consists of header, payload, and trailer. The header contains information reletive to source and destination addresses, protocols, and packet number, the payload contains the actual byte data being sent in the packet, and the trailer contains data to show the end of the packet and error correction.
